The sun sets on my city  
Later than yours  
The air I breathe  
Is not filled with smoke and cinder  
  
The men whose business war is  
Allow us to see  
Your suffering  
While hiding the suffering we cause  
  
The righteous choose suffering  
As a path  
For others  
Blind to their own sunset  
  
Sun falls  
Darkness comes  
The final sunset   
For some  
  
The final darkness  
For all
